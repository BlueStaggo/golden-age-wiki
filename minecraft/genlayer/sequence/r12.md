    ieq1-->|No|loop4repeat
    ieq1-->|Yes|shore["`**Shore**`"]
    shore-->swampRivers1000["`**Swamp Rivers**
    Seed: 1000`"]

    Seed: 1000`"]

    Seed: 10`"]
    riverMix--->output0(["`**Biomes for World Generation**`"])
